Bartek Podkowa

Director/Producer

Bartek Podkowa spent years studying to be a scientist at the University of Cambridge, King’s College London and University College London. But one day he found himself looking for a movie he wanted to watch… but which didn’t exist. Very soon he discovered how important filmmaking is to him, and how much impact it can have on a person’s life. Now he spends most of his time writing, filming, editing and building Seven Hills into a positive voice in the film industry. And he’s still trying to make the movie that started it all.
